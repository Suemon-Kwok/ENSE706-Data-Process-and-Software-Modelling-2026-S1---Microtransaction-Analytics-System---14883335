Relationship Types

Association
Classes know each other. Solid + open arrow.
VP → Relationship → Association
Inheritance
Child extends parent. Solid + hollow triangle.
VP → Relationship → Generalization
Composition
Strong part-of. Filled ◆ at owner end.
VP → Relationship → Composition
Aggregation
Weak has-a. Open ◇ at container end.
VP → Relationship → Aggregation
Realization
Implements interface. Dashed + hollow triangle.
VP → Relationship → Realization
Dependency
Temporary use. Dashed + open arrow. Label: «uses»
VP → Relationship → Dependency
